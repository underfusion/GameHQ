What is GameHQ?

GameHQ brings one coherent capture workflow to PC gaming. Tap one button for a screenshot, hold it after something memorable happens to save recent gameplay, and open a gallery above the running game without alt-tabbing. Captures are identified, grouped, and filed automatically per game.

At its heart is a configurable rolling replay buffer: while you play, GameHQ encodes recent gameplay with system audio into crash-safe segments on disk. Hold Share and those previous minutes become a normal H.264 MP4 with a thumbnail and game metadata. Tap Share for an instant PNG or JPEG, or focus a recorded clip and press Share or S to save the exact displayed video frame as a screenshot.

The in-game overlay supports browsing, playback, favorites, reveal, and deletion from a controller. It refreshes immediately after new captures and automatically closes when Windows focus moves to Start, the task switcher, or another application. The desktop gallery adds thumbnail zoom, bulk selection, watched folders, and a unified view of Game Bar, Steam, NVIDIA, and OBS captures without modifying imported files.

GameHQ 0.6.3 includes thirteen live-switchable themes with Obsidian as the fresh-install default and adjustable overlay dimming. Input supports DualSense, XInput, WinMM, keyboard, and safe extra mouse buttons with fully configurable bindings. If HidHide makes a connected Sony controller invisible to applications, Settings explains the cause and can safely add GameHQ to the existing allow-list.

GameHQ is a portable native C++20/Qt 6 app — no Electron, account, telemetry, background service, or game-process injection. It uses Windows Graphics Capture, Media Foundation, and WASAPI directly, and can keep its settings, database, logs, and captures beside the executable.
