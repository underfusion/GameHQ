# GameHQ name and branding

The GameHQ name, logo, and visual identity identify builds and services
published by the GameHQ project. This document is a project branding policy;
it does not claim that any mark is registered and it does not change or add a
restriction to the MIT-licensed software.

You may make accurate descriptive references to GameHQ, link to the project,
state compatibility, and preserve factual attribution. Unmodified official
artifacts may retain their original name and branding.

Modified or independently distributed builds must not imply that they are
official, approved, supported, or signed by the GameHQ project. Unless the
maintainer gives written permission, use a distinct product name and visual
identity for such builds while retaining the copyright and license notices
required by their software licenses.

Forks may truthfully say that they are based on or compatible with GameHQ.
They may not use GameHQ branding in a way likely to confuse users about the
source, publisher, security review, or support of the build.

Questions or permission requests should use the repository's public issue
tracker without posting private identity or account information.
